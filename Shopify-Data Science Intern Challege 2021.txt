i) How many orders were shipped by Speedy Express in total?

Ans: 
SELECT COUNT(*) AS TotalOrders
FROM [Orders] AS O, [Shippers] AS S
WHERE O.ShipperId = S.ShipperId AND ShipperName = "Speedy Express";

There were total 54 orders shipped by Speedy Express.

ii) What is the last name of the employee with the most orders?

Ans: 
SELECT [Employees].LastName, COUNT(*) AS TotalOrders
FROM [Orders]
JOIN [Employees]
ON [Orders].EmployeeID = [Employees].EmployeeID
GROUP BY [Employees].LastName
ORDER BY TotalOrders DESC
LIMIT 1

Employee with the last name "Peacock" made the most orders 40.

iii) What product was ordered the most by customers in Germany?

Ans: 
SELECT [Products].ProductName, SUM([OrderDetails].Quantity) AS "TotalOrdered"
FROM ([Orders] JOIN [Customers] ON [Customers].CustomerID = [Orders].CustomerID
               JOIN [OrderDetails] ON [OrderDetails].OrderID = [Orders].OrderID
               JOIN [Products] ON [Products].ProductID = [OrderDetails].ProductID)
WHERE [Customers].Country = 'Germany'
GROUP BY [OrderDetails].ProductID

In Germany, Boston Crab Meat was ordered the most(160) by customers.
